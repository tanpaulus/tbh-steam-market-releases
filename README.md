# 🎒 TBH Steam Market

**See what your stash in _TBH: Task Bar Hero_ is worth — in seconds, without opening Steam and without typing anything.**

A free, read-only desktop app for Windows. It reads your TBH stash and shows **live Steam Community Market prices and buy orders**, so you know exactly what you own and what you could sell it for right now.

## ✨ What it does

| | Feature |
|---|---|
| 💰 | **Total stash value** — gear + materials, read straight from your save. No manual entry. |
| 💸 | **Buy orders** — see who wants to buy your items and for how much, so you can **sell instantly**. |
| 🔎 | **Deep Scan** — checks every item one by one, catching things that have buyers but no sell listing. |
| 🪙 | **Auto currency** — shows prices in **your Steam currency** automatically (Rupiah, USD, etc.). |
| ⚡ | **Sort by demand or value** — find what sells fastest, or what's worth the most, at a glance. |
| 🔄 | **Auto-updates itself** — new versions install in the background, no reinstalling. |

> Built specifically for **TBH: Task Bar Hero** — it finds and reads your stash automatically.

## ⬇ Install (2 minutes)

1. **[Download the latest version](../../releases/latest)** — grab the file named **`TBH-Steam-Market-Setup-x.x.x.exe`**.
2. Run it. Windows may show **"Windows protected your PC"** — this is the normal warning for any free app that isn't code-signed (paid certificate), **not** a problem with this app. Click **"More info" → "Run anyway"**.
3. Done! The app opens in its own window, finds your stash automatically, and **updates itself** whenever a new version ships.

> 💡 Use the **`Setup`** installer (the one with `Setup` in the name) — not the `portable` `.exe` — so auto-update works.
>
> 🕹️ First time? Open TBH at least once so the game creates its save, then launch the app and your stash fills in automatically.

## ✅ Is it safe? Will I get banned?

**No.** This app **only READS** files already on your computer — like opening a file in Notepad.

- ✅ Reads a **copy** of your save (in memory) just to calculate value.
- ✅ Uses the same public price data the Steam website uses.
- ❌ Does **not** modify your save or touch the game while it runs.
- ❌ Does **not** automate buying or selling.
- ❌ Does **not** send your data anywhere — it runs **100% on your PC**.

Bans happen when people **modify** saves to cheat or automate trades. None of that happens here.

> ⚠️ Use at your own risk. This is an independent project — not affiliated with Valve or the TBH developers.

## 🆘 Quick help

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| **"TBH save not found"** | Open the TBH game once (so it creates the save), then click **Refresh** in the app. |
| Item shows **"no listing"** | Normal — Steam has no active **sell** listing for it right now. Click **💸 View buy orders** to find an instant buyer. |
| Windows blocked the install | See step 2 above — **"More info" → "Run anyway"**. It's the standard unsigned-app prompt. |
